# CoBnB KSA Reference Website - Complete Design Analysis

## Color Palette (EXACT)
- **Primary Background**: Dark navy/slate #0f172a (hero, "how it works", footer)
- **Secondary Background**: Light gray #f1f5f9 (services section)
- **Accent Color**: Teal/Cyan #14b8a6 (buttons, underlines, icons, badges)
- **Text on Dark**: White #ffffff
- **Text on Light**: Dark #1e293b
- **Card Background**: White #ffffff with subtle shadow
- **Navbar**: Dark navy #0f172a with white text, sticky
- **Stats numbers**: Teal #14b8a6
- **Step numbers (01,02,03)**: Muted gray/teal with low opacity

## Typography
- Arabic: Bold, large headings (likely Tajawal or similar)
- Section titles: Centered, bold, with teal underline accent below
- Body text: Regular weight, gray tones

## Layout Structure
1. **Navbar**: Sticky dark navy, logo right (RTL), menu center, auth buttons left
   - Items: الرئيسية, من نحن, خدماتنا, العقارات, CoBnB+, لملاك العقارات, المدونة, تواصل معنا
   - Language toggle (EN), تسجيل الدخول (outline), حساب جديد (teal filled)
   
2. **Hero**: Full-width dark navy bg, right-aligned text (RTL)
   - Small badge: "الآن في المملكة العربية السعودية" with green dot
   - Large heading in white
   - Subtitle in lighter gray
   - Two CTAs: "أدرج عقارك" (teal filled with arrow), "احجز إقامتك" (teal outline)
   
3. **Stats Bar**: 4 animated counters on white/light bg
   - Numbers in teal, labels in dark gray
   
4. **Services**: Light gray bg, "خدماتنا" centered title with teal underline
   - 6 cards in 3x2 grid, white bg, teal icons, hover effects
   - "عرض جميع الخدمات" button
   
5. **How It Works**: Dark navy bg, "كيف يعمل" centered
   - 3 steps: 01, 02, 03 in large muted numbers
   - RTL layout (01 on right)
   
6. **Revenue Model**: 70/30 split display
   
7. **Featured Properties**: Property cards with images, badges "مميز"
   - Price per night, bedrooms/bathrooms/guests icons
   
8. **Cities**: 3 city cards with emoji icons and district counts
   
9. **Testimonials**: Quote cards with customer names and locations
   
10. **CTA Section**: "حقق أقصى استفادة من عقارك"
    
11. **Partners**: Airbnb, Booking.com, Agoda logos

12. **WhatsApp**: Green floating button bottom-left

## Key Design Patterns
- Teal underline below section titles
- Dark/light alternating sections
- Cards with subtle shadows, no heavy borders
- Teal accent for all interactive elements
- Clean, minimal, modern aesthetic
- RTL-first layout throughout
